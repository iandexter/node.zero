Orpheus ascending


The resonance of silence mingles with your breath.
You lay there cold, doomed to the clutches of the
blinding darkness around you. The trap

is laid bare before you, enticing
both prey and hunter. Then my song.

Slowly, consciousness cloaks the void,
unraveling the lyric longing from within
me. You flow through the rhythms

hidden between the discordant howls of forever.
I looked back and you are gone.
